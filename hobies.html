Rescue Dogs

I've always had dogs as a part of my life since I was a child and having one of my parents work as a vet we often had stray dogs come and stay with us.
When I moved to South Korea I began fostering shelter dogs who needed a place at the risk of being euthanized. So myself and my girlfrien signed up to be foster carers for the dogs until they could find long term houses
Recently I have set up an charity page to create NFTs for shelter dogs. This allows people who do not have the time or space to actually adopt a dog be able to contribute in some way to the shelter and also owning a virtual dog. This project is still in the very early stages but you can view the current NFTs on Opensea
